Why choose Good gums

Our nursery is open to the public from April through October. We offer a variety of services, including landscape design, installation, and maintenance. We are proud to have been voted "Best Nursery" in the Harrisonburg area for the past six years.

Good Gums Nursery is a full-service nursery that specialises in trees, shrubs, and perennials. We grow a wide variety of plants, including many native species.
